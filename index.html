Benefits of PDR

  • Since your car does not need to be repainted during PDR–it is incredibly affordable for those without insurance or with an already totaled out car.
  • PDR is incredibly fast since no part of your car is removed–usually done in a single day.
  • Since PDR uses no paint–it has no negative effects on the environment. Meaning it is a great way to keep Denver green!
  • Almost all paintless dent repair is covered by insurance companies. In fact, many times there is no out of pocket expense for you at all!
